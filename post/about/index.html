Kaspa Hub: The Central Hub for Kaspa Ecosystem Developments

In a world of rapid technological change, finding a reliable, community-focused place to explore, learn, and connect across open protocols can be challenging. That is where Kaspa Hub comes in.

We are a community-built, open-source platform dedicated to helping you navigate ecosystems built around freedom, decentralization, and accessibility. Whether you are a newcomer discovering these technologies or an experienced builder contributing to them, Kaspa Hub connects you with resources, tools, and people driving meaningful progress.

Why We Exist

Kaspa Hub was launched on June 2, 2025 with no corporate backing. Our mission is to make the Kaspa protocol accessible, understandable, and practical for everyone - from curious beginners to seasoned developers and contributors.

Kaspa Hub is funded through community donations and website advertisements, ensuring we remain fully independent and aligned with our users.

Our Philosophy

  • Open and Neutral - We remain fully independent, not affiliated with the Kaspa core team, and do not favor any single group or team.
  • Education First - Complex concepts are presented in an approachable and clear manner.
  • Community Powered - User contributions are welcomed, encouraged, and celebrated.
  • FOSS Principles - We are deeply committed to free and open-source software (FOSS) principles. Every resource we share and feature we develop supports sustainable growth, transparency, and user empowerment in the Kaspa ecosystem.

What is Kaspa

Kaspa is a high-performance proof-of-work Layer-1 blockDAG that utilizes the revolutionary GHOSTDAG consensus protocol and blockDAG architecture. This design enables parallel block creation, delivering exceptional scalability and near-instant transaction confirmations while maintaining full decentralization and robust security.

Currently operating at 10 blocks per second with average transaction finality times of approximately 7 seconds, Kaspa offers a powerful foundation for fast, secure value transfer. Powered by the efficient kHeavyHash algorithm and launched fairly in November 2021 without premines or allocations, network continues to evolve with ambitious development goals, including higher block rates and advanced smart contract capabilities.

Kaspa Hub serves as your central resource by curating the best ecosystem tools: block explorers and blockDAG visualizers, wallets, exchanges, developer documentation, payment solutions, analytics platforms, news updates, merchant directories, and community initiatives.

We are constantly evolving - contributions and ideas are always welcome!
