Nostr Tools

A collection of tools for the Nostr users.

Nostr Clients Directory

Browse and compare popular Nostr client applications. Find the one that best suits your preferences and usage.

Create Nostr Account

Easily create a Nostr account by generating your private key. Save it securely and use it to log in, manage your profile, and connect across the Nostr network.

Nostr Account Settings

Edit your Nostr profile to share your identity by adding information such as your username, bio, Bitcoin Lightning Network address, and Kaspa wallet.

Nostr Raw Data Viewer

View raw Nostr user data directly from multiple relays. Enter your public key (npub or hex) to fetch events, kind 0 profiles, and explore decentralized identity with Kaspa integration.
